Technical SEO Audit

  • Whether you are looking to solve traffic issues or faster growth, a website audit is the place
    to start to get SEO clarity.

    An audit and action plan is custom tailored to your unique situation. Its goal is to identify issues and opportunities and prioritize them.

    Areas covered in an audit:

    • Indexing
    • Accessibility
    • Architecture & Usability
    • On Page Elements
    • Keywords & Topical Optimization
    • Content Review
    • Analytics Review
    • Inbound links
    • Competitive Analysis

    Audits are based on a checklist assembled over years of hands-on experience, the generous contribution of Annie Cushing checklist, and other industry leaders.

    Audit Cost

    The cost of an audit depends on site complexity, size and what is covered. It usually range between $2,000 and $5,000.

    An audit consist of several hours of deep analysis of your website. This is not an automated audit that you can get for free or a smaller fee.
